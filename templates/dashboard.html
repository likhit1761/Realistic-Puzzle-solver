About

The game first appeared in Japan in 1984 where it was given the name “Sudoku” which is short for a longer expression in Japanese – “Sūji wa dokushin ni kagiru” – which means, “the digits are limited to one occurrence.”.

This is based on the logical placement of numbers. A game of logic, Sudoku doesn’t require any calculation nor special math skills; all that is needed are brains and concentration.

Explore

Explore the Unknown

Never stop your anxiety and explore. The more you explore is the more you grab. The world is in yours fingertips.

I started designing project plan, so that I can focus on that untill the end and came up with real time sudoku solver where one can scan sudoku, and get its solution in a very less time than actually solving it manually. I made it user-friendly by adding much more functionalities like hint, reset and clear specific cell. As an extension to the project a random sudoku board is added, where user can simply solve the various sudoku and visualized how actually the backtracking works to take out solution of sudoku
